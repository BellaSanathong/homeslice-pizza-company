Keepin' it Easy

Home Slice Pizza Company is a kitchen offering take away pizza in the EIT building at UALR. Home Slice Pizza uses fresh local produce with the thoughts of students in mind. In order to help students eat better and live better, Home Slice has taken a step to make the lives of all college students a little easier.

Meet the Chef

Donatello Scarsi (age 78) first developed his culinary skills by producing and selling cheese. It was in Naples, the birthplace of pizza just an hour away from his hometown (Pontinia, Italy), where Scarsi went to study the art and craft of the pizzaiolo.

After learning all things Neapolitan, among some of the best pizza makers in the world, Scarsi came to the United States and opened several successful pizzerias before settling in Little Rock Arkansas.
